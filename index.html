Quick start

Your first hour

You wake at anchor in Heron Cove. The checklist quest walks you through weighing anchor, running the marked channel to Miller's Landing, docking — docking mode is your friend, pods swing ±90° and split throttle — buying provisions, and taking your first courier gig.

After that the Reach is yours: a lake, a winding river with real current, a tidal bay, three marinas, and a fishing pole waiting on Driftwood Beach. Watch the power-flow card — solar in, loads out, the battery is the story of your day.

Beyond the checklist, the Reach keeps going: kinematic traffic shares the water (avoiding is on you), marinas sell boat upgrades, three newer coves turn up discovery events and cove evenings ashore, a fat-tire e-scooter gets you around on land, and courier gigs sometimes call for an actual tow behind a visible dinghy. The WX radio speaks forecasts and alerts aloud.

The mouse works differently in each mode. Walking, it's captured automatically — move it and the view follows, like any first-person game (a stray Esc lets go; one click grabs it back). At the helm it's freed for the dashboard — sliders, the chart, switches — so right-click and hold is what glances you around, the way you'd actually turn your head from the wheel.
